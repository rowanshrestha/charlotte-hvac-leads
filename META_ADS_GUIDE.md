# Meta Ads Guide — Charlotte HVAC Lead Gen

## Campaign Setup

**Objective:** Leads (not traffic — this gets you the cheapest form fills)
**Budget:** $15/day to start
**Platform:** Facebook + Instagram (both checked)

---

## Targeting

**Location:** Charlotte, NC + 25 mile radius
**Age:** 28–65
**Homeowners only:** Yes — under Detailed Targeting add "Homeowners"
**Exclude:** Renters, apartment dwellers

**Additional interests to layer (pick 2-3):**
- Home improvement
- HVAC / Air conditioning
- Home repair
- Homeownership

## Creative (Images/Video)

**What works best:**
1. Before/after photos of HVAC installs
2. Photo of a technician working on an AC unit (real, not stock)
3. Simple graphic: "FREE HVAC Quote — Charlotte, NC" on orange background
4. Short video (15s): "Broken AC? We'll get you a free quote in 60 seconds"

**Colors:** Use orange (#f97316) — matches landing page, builds brand recognition

---

## Conversion Setup

1. Create a Meta Pixel on your landing page (already included in the HTML)
2. Set up a "Lead" custom conversion — fires when someone reaches the thank-you state
3. Use "Conversions" objective once you have 10+ leads (Meta needs data first)
4. Start with "Traffic" or "Leads" objective for first week

---

## Budget Scaling Rules

| Weekly leads | Action |
|---|---|
| 0–3 leads | Change ad creative, test new headline |
| 4–8 leads | Hold budget, optimize landing page |
| 9–15 leads | Scale to $20/day |
| 15+ leads | Scale to $30/day, duplicate campaign for new zip codes |

## Finding Contractors to Sell Leads To

Search Google for: `HVAC contractor Charlotte NC`
Look for businesses with:
- A website (means they're established)
- Reviews on Google (active business)
- No lead generation service already listed

**How to pitch them:**
"Hi, I run a lead generation service for HVAC contractors in Charlotte.
I have homeowners actively requesting quotes right now.
I sell exclusive leads for $50–75 each — first come first served.
Want me to send you the next one as a free trial?"

Start with a free trial lead → they close it → they're a customer for life.
